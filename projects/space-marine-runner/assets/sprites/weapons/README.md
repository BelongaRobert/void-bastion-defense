## Recommended Sources:

1. **Kenney.nl/weapon-pack** - Excellent free weapon sprites
2. **Kenney.nl/particle-pack** - Muzzle flash, bullet effects
3. **OpenGameArt.org** - "gun", "weapon", "sci-fi weapon"

## Format:

- **PNG** with transparency
- **Weapons**: 48x24 or 64x32
- **Projectiles**: 8x8 to 16x16
- **Effects**: 16x16 to 32x32
