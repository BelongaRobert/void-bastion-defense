## About This Game

### Extended Description

The enemy approaches. The Void Bastion, last fortress of humanity in this sector, stands alone against the endless tide.

**Void Bastion Defense** is a hardcore tower defense action game set in a grim, war-torn universe inspired by classic military sci-fi. Command the defense of the Bastion against relentless waves of Tyranid swarms, Chaos cultists, and ancient Necron horrors.

### Core Features

**BUILD YOUR DEFENSE**
Deploy over 30 unique defensive towers, each with distinct capabilities and upgrade paths. From precision sniper turrets that eliminate high-value targets at extreme range, to devastating plasma coils that tear through clustered enemies, to rapid-fire heavy bolters that saturate the battlefield with firepower. Every tower matters. Every placement counts.

**FACE THE HORDE**
Battle through endless waves of increasingly deadly enemies. Each wave brings new challenges: swifter xenos, heavily armored elites, and titanic boss creatures that will test your strategic mastery to its limits. Five distinct enemy factions, each with unique behaviors, strengths, and weaknesses to exploit.

**MASTER THE UPGRADE**
Invest your war chest in tower upgrades that transform basic defenses into engines of destruction. Unlock advanced firing modes, expanded range, and devastating special abilities. Chain upgrades together to create devastating kill zones.

**SURVIVE THE IMPOSSIBLE**
There's no "winning" in the Void Bastion—only surviving longer than the last commander. How many waves can you hold? 50? 100? The leaderboard awaits your legend.

### Game Modes

**Campaign Mode**
Learn the art of defense through 20 handcrafted scenarios, each teaching new strategies and tower combinations. Face unique challenges and unlock the full arsenal of the Bastion.

**Endless Mode**
The true test. How long can you survive against infinite waves? Compete on global leaderboards for eternal glory.

**Weekly Challenges**
Special modifiers, limited resources, unique rules. New challenges every week with exclusive rewards for the victors.

### Steam Features

- **30 Achievements** to unlock, including secrets for the most dedicated commanders
- **Global Leaderboards** - compete with defenders worldwide
- **Steam Cloud** - your progress follows you across devices
- **Trading Cards** - collect and craft badges
- **Steam Workshop** - share and download custom scenarios (planned)
