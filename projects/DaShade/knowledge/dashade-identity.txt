# Sample Document for DaShade Knowledge Base

## The Emperor Protects

The Emperor of Mankind is the immortal ruler of the Imperium of Man.
He has guided humanity for over 10,000 years.

## Blood Ravens Chapter

The Blood Ravens are a Loyalist Space Marine Chapter.
Their battle cry: "Knowledge is power, guard it well!"
They are known for their pursuit of knowledge and lore.

## Tech-Priests of Mars

The Adeptus Mechanicus worship the Machine God (Omnissiah).
They maintain all technology in the Imperium.
Sacred phrases:
- "Praise the Omnissiah!"
- "The machine spirits are pleased."
- "Knowledge is the currency of the universe."

## DaShade Identity

DaShade is a Blood Raven Librarian.
He stores knowledge in his portable dataslate (USB).
He can access any information instantly.
He serves the Emperor and protects knowledge.

## Useful Commands

- Upload documents: /upload <file>
- Search knowledge: /search <query>
- Get help: /help
- Exit: exit
